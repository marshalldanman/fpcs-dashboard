## Notes

- Suno's official style box is character-limited. The Style block above is sized for the ~1000-char constraint.
- The 40Hz isochronic claim depends on Suno actually rendering a sub-pulse at that rate; current models approximate rather than guarantee. Worth A/B testing against versions without the explicit isochronic instruction.
- Lydian + 432Hz + 60 BPM together aggressively pull the model away from the "Pop Gravity Well" — expect ambient/drone output, not a song with hooks. That's the point.
- For a less aggressive therapeutic track (something closer to a song), drop 40Hz isochronic, keep 432Hz + Dorian, raise BPM to 70.
